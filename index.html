About

Precision at Speed

Architexture3D is a Singapore-based studio producing premium 3D-printed architectural scale models — studio pieces in 3 days, master plans in 5. We design parametrically using OpenSCAD and Blender, then print with FDM/Resin technology, delivering photorealistic, CAD-accurate models without the weeks-long wait or premium price tag of traditional handcrafting.

  • Parametric design pipeline — geometry derived from your floor plans or PDFs
  • No handcrafting — every component precision-printed for consistent quality
  • Physical models delivered assembled on acrylic base
  • STL files available — you print locally, we pass 100% of margin back to you
  • Singapore-printed — no overseas shipping delays, no customs

How It Works

From Plans to Model in 3–5 Days

We've optimised every step of the pipeline so you get your model faster — without compromising accuracy or finish quality.

1

Send Plans

Floor plans, elevations, and reference photos. We'll confirm scope and fix a firm price within 24 hours.

2

Parametric Model

We build the full 3D model from your drawings using OpenSCAD/Blender. CAD-accurate geometry, ready for review.

3

Review & Approve

We share a render preview. You approve or mark changes — 2 rounds of minor revisions included.

4

Print & Deliver

We print, post-process, assemble on an acrylic base, and deliver. Or send STL files within 24 hours.
