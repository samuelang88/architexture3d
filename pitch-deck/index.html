The Solution

Precision at Speed, at 1/3 the Cost

Architexture3D replaces handcrafting with a parametric digital pipeline — extracting geometry directly from floor plans, printing with FDM/Resin, and assembling with jig-aligned precision.

🔧

Parametric Design

Floor plans and PDFs go straight into OpenSCAD/Blender. Geometry is CAD-accurate, revision-friendly, and reusable. No manual tracing. No errors from hand-transfer.

🖨️

Digital Fabrication

Every component is precision-printed on industrial FDM/Resin printers. Consistent quality, no skill variance, zero rework. Print farm economics mean 95%+ margins per machine.

✋

Hand-Finished Assembly

Post-processing, paint, and assembly on acrylic base. Photorealistic quality indistinguishable from $15,000 handcrafted models — at a fraction of the cost and time.
